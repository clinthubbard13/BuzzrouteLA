BuzzRoutes of the Week

Downtown LA Dive Tour

  • Bar Jackalope – Whiskey speakeasy
  • The Escondite – Grungy patio, burgers
  • Golden Gopher – Arcade machines + cocktails

Walk Time: 15 mins total

Bonus: Stop at Grand Central Market for tacos between bars 1 and 2.

Silver Lake Sunset Hop

  • Thirsty Crow – Bourbon bar
  • Bar Flores – Mezcal rooftop
  • The Friend – DJ sets, neon vibes

Walk Time: 10 mins total

Things to Do: Street art selfie spots + pizza at Garage Pizza

Venice Vibes Crawl

  • High Rooftop Lounge – Ocean views
  • Hinano Cafe – Divey burger joint
  • The Brig – Trendy crowd, food trucks

Walk Time: 12 mins total

Bonus: Chill at the skate park or walk the boardwalk between drinks.
